Environmental Impact Assessment-

It is a process of evaluating the likely environmental impacts of a proposed project or development, taking into account inter-related socio-economic, cultural, and human-health impacts, both beneficial and adverse. EIA is a tool used to assess the positive and negative environmental, economic, and social impacts of a project.

Agronomic studies& crop water requirement-

Crop water requirements (CWR) are defined as the depth of water (millimeters) needed to meet the water consumed through evapotranspiration (ETc) by a disease-free crop, growing in large fields under nonrestricting soil conditions, including soil water and fertility, and achieving full production potential
